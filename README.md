#Zero Gravity (indev)
Welcome to a zero-gravity world where gravity is not fixed!
In this game, you can switch gravity up and down to move through obstacles and reach the goal.

?️ How to Play

Use the controls to move your character

Switch gravity to walk on the ceiling or floor

Avoid obstacles and time your switches carefully

✨ Features

Unique gravity-switch mechanic

Zero-gravity themed gameplay

Challenging levels that test your timing and thinking

Can you master gravity and beat the level?
